About the author

Keith Murphy is a software developer, trainer, and voice actor from Boston, Massachusetts.  He has been with Wakefly since 2011.  His prior experience includes insurance, restaurant, finance, customer service and marketing.  He also pursued professional wrestling for seven years which spawns a side business as a fight choreographer for various theaters in New England.

Today Keith can be hired for consultant work or projects.  These typically involve voice acting, software development or fight choreography.

When he's not working, Keith is usually following one his other passions.  He enjoys singing, acting, video games, hiking and traveling. 

He can be reached via email at keith@murfbard.com.
