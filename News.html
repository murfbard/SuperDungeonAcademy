About the Game

Before heroes get a chance to appear in other stories they first have to be trained and battle tested. Enter Super Dungeon Academy!

Create your very own superhero, swordsman, or wizard and take him through a challenging adventure! Super Dungeon Academy is a randomly generated action adventure game that is sure to challenge and delight.
